Francisco Bangoy National High School
Senior High School

Francisco Bangoy National High School is a public school started in 1969 as a Barrio Extension High School of Davao City Nat'l High School. In 1973, it became Barangay High School when it was subsidized by the City Government of Davao under the leadership of the newly installed Mayor Elias B. Lopez. In 1988, putting up of the first public high school in Sasa was realized. It was named FRANCISCO BANGOY HIGH SCHOOL due to urgent need to cater the growing high school population having only DCNHS. The late Paciano V. Bangoy, son of the renowned Francisco B. Bangoy, donated 1 hectare-lot which was utilized for the general purpose of putting up a school (both elem. and secondary) in the area. In 1988, by virtue of Republic Act No. 6655 known as the FREE SECONDARY EDUCATION ACT of 1988, all barangay high schools were converted into NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOLS in which funding for the School's operation and teacher's salary would since then be provided by the national government. Due to its growing population with limited area (2,849 sq. m.), FBNHS has two annex campuses in Lanang (1990) and Malagamot (1994). However, the Lanang campus was GRANTED AUTONOMY and is now known as the RAMOS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL. The latter DR. SANTIAGO DAKUDAO SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL's autonomy is on process of legislation.

Enrollment Requirements and Procedures

Step 1: Proceed to the Senior High School campus located at the KM 9 Paradise Drive, Sasa, Davao City for submission of requirements.
Step 2: Show your printed or screenshot copy of your enrollment receipt from the online registration.
Step 3: Submit the original copy of the school requirements found in the portal.
Step 4: List the name of your section and class adviser.
Step 5: Check your messenger for an invitation to your respective class group chat (GC).
Step 6: Wait for further instructions to be posted in the GC.
Step 7: Come back for the opening of class.
